LIbrary Daze

Hello! My name is Kayla Hamilton, and I will be your new school librarian this upcoming school year, 2025-2026 .

I am hoping to incorporate a community environment within the library. I want to document my journey as I make a switch from "just checking in and out books" to creating a place in which students, teachers, parents, and the community work together for the betterment of our children. I want everyone to have a role in which to make the library and school successful. To learn more about me, vist my All About Me Page.
